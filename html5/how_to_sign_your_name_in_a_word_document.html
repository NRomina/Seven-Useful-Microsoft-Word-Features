How to Sign Your Name in a Word Document

Learn how to add your signature in a Microsoft Word document.

  1. First, click on the Insert tab above the toolbar. Next, in the Lines section of the Shapes dropdown menu, click on Scribble.
  2. After you click on Scribble, your cursor turns into a drawing tool. Finally, drag your mouse to sign your name.
You have signed your name in your Word document.
