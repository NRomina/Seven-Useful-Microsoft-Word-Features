How to Protect Documents with a Password

In case of secret business documents or personal documents with private information, it makes sense to protect the contents of Word documents with a password. In this section, you will learn how to do it.

  1. On the File tab, go to Info, and then Protect Dcocument.
  2. A dropdown menu opens. Click on Encrypt with Password.
  3. An Encrypt Document dialogue box opens for you to type your password. Then, hit Ok.
Your Word document is password-protected.
