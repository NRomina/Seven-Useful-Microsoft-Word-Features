How to Look Up Unfamiliar Words or Phrases

Whenever you don't know the meaning of a word or phrase, you can resort to the Smart Lookup feature of Microsoft Word to check it. In this section, you will find out how.

  1. Select the word or phrase.
  2. Right-click the selected term, then choose Search.
  3. The results pane opens showing more information about that word or phrase, including definitions, Wikipedia articles, and top related searches from the web.
You are able to learn the meaning of a particular word or phrase.
